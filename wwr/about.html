Our mission is to provide a safe and enjoyable experience for all of our customers. We have made our motto "Safety First" and we take that very seriously. History

The Western Whitewater Rafting company was founded in 1999 by a group of friends who had a passion for whitewater rafting. They wanted to share their love of the river with others and provide a safe and enjoyable experience for all who wanted to join them. They started with just a few rafts and a small group of guides. They have grown over the years and have become one of the premier rafting companies in the western United States. They have expanded their operations to include trips on some of the most exciting rivers in the country. They have also expanded their fleet of rafts and have added more guides to their team. They are excited to continue to grow and provide the best possible experience for their customers.
